Student Daily Health Form

All students enrolled at CU Boulder must fill out this form each day they plan to be on campus for both general or academic purposes.


Student Employee Daily Health Form

All student employeeds working on campus (include student researchers) must fill out this form each day they are scheduled to work on campus. Students must also fill out the Student Daily Health Form.


Faculty & Staff Daily Health Form

All Faculty, Staff, & Researchers must fill out this form each day they are scheduled to work on campus.
